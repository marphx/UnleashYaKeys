How to Type Chords

UnleashYaKeys reads chord symbols flexibly — multiple notations work for the same chord. Tap a section to expand it.

```
Root Notes ▶

Use letters A–G with optional sharps or flats:

  • C, D, E, F, G, A, B — natural notes
  • C# or Db — accidentals (sharp/flat are interchangeable)
  • Unicode ♯ and ♭ are also accepted
Triads & Quality ▶
  • C — major (just the root)
  • Cm · Cmin · C- — minor
  • Cdim · C° — diminished
  • Caug · C+ — augmented
  • Csus2 — suspended 2nd
  • Csus4 · Csus — suspended 4th
  • C5 — power chord (root & 5th)
7th Chords ▶
  • C7 — dominant 7
  • Cmaj7 · CM7 · CΔ — major 7
  • Cm7 · Cmin7 · C-7 — minor 7
  • CmMaj7 · Cm(maj7) — minor major 7
  • Cdim7 · C°7 — full diminished 7
  • Cm7b5 · Cø — half diminished
  • Caug7 · C7#5 — augmented 7
  • C7sus4 — dominant 7 sus 4
Extensions: 9 · 11 · 13 ▶
  • C9 · Cmaj9 · Cm9 — 9th chords
  • C11 · Cmaj11 · Cm11 — 11ths
  • C13 · Cmaj13 · Cm13 — 13ths
  • Cadd9 · Cm(add9) — adds without the 7th
  • C6 · Cm6 · C6/9 · Cm6/9
Alterations · ♯ & ♭ ▶

Alterations stack right after the chord type:

  • C7b9 · C7#9 — altered 9
  • C7#11 · Cmaj7#11 — Lydian sound
  • C7b13 · C7#5 — altered 5/13
  • C9b5 · C9#5 · C9#11
  • C13b9 · C13#9 · C13#11
  • C7alt — altered dominant (♭9 ♯9 ♯11 ♭13)
  • Combined: C7b9b13, C7#9#11, C7b9#11
Slash Chords / Bass ▶

Add a bass note with / followed by the note name:

  • C/E — C major over E (1st inversion)
  • Cmaj7/G — Cmaj7 over G
  • Dm7/G — Dm7 over G (a G9sus sound)
  • F/G — F triad over G (G9sus polychord)

Tip: C6/9 is parsed as a chord type, not a slash — the parser knows.

Special Symbols ▶
  • Δ = maj7 (so CΔ = Cmaj7)
  • ° = diminished (C° = Cdim)
  • ø = half-diminished (Cø = Cm7b5)
  • + = augmented (C+ = Caug)
  • - = minor (C-7 = Cm7)
  • ♯ / ♭ = sharp / flat
```

Keyboard Shortcuts

Connect a Bluetooth or USB keyboard. Click anywhere on the app first to give it focus, then use these keys. (When the chord input field is focused, only Enter / Escape work — click off the field for the rest.)

